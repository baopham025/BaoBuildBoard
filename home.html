ABOUT ME

Bao Build Boards was founded in 2022 by a keyboard enthusiast who name is Bao. He started Bao Build Boards with a goal that make the hobby become more accessible to everyone. The hobby can be overwhelmed for people who have never know about mechanical keyboard but we will try our best to everyone try out the hobby as fun as possible.

Our goals are providing excellent customer service, making it easy for beginners to access the hobby and offering unbeatable prices and cheapest shipping options. We offer a variety of custom keyboard and deskmat options to our customers. We also build custom keyboard base on your choices and your references with our experience in the hobby. We hope that we can bring the hobby to everyone so we can create a community here in London, Ontario.
